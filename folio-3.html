Life Planets

2020

This work is the main Key Visual and extended images for the "3rd Anniversary Celebration of Raffles City Shenzhen" in 2020. The brand tone of Raffles is young, trendy, and full of vitality. This work takes planet roving as the theme and depicts a scene where a group of young and fashionable people are holding a party on Raffles Planet.
